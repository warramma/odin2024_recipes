Resources for productivity and learning

  • Ali Abdaal: Ali Abdaal is one my favorite resources to learn new productivity methods, not just for school but life in general. He also recently released a great book called Feel Good Productivity which builds on lessons from his Youtube channel.
  • Studyquill: great introduction into the science of learning, as well as fun vids about stationary, hobbies, and personal finance for young adults.
  • Elizabeth Filips: Great summaries of research on neuroscience, psychology, and productivity. Also interesting neurodivergent perspective with great alternative approaches to popular producitivity methods.

Favorite internet things

  • TLDR newsletter: one of my favorite tech newsletters to stay up to date, with more focused newsletters available on webdev, ai, infosec, etc

tips for using & finding resources

  1. click on anything that interests you
  2. don't fall into the trap of watching productivty content just to feel productive lol
